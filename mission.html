Our Mission

Unlocking Industry Potential

Industry-Inflection
Big data. IoT. Software-Defined Networking. The list of (relatively) new, important and disruptive technologies goes on and on. It proves an undeniable fact: we are at an inflection point in many of our technology industries. Networks are changing — the need for data, security and visibility is at an all time high. Hardware is transitioning — we’re looking for ways to inject as much intelligence as possible into devices, while simultaneously reducing operational and maintenance costs. Collaboration is essential, but requires encryption and accessibility.

Overall, it boils down to the fact that cost-effective and well-developed software is where the global community is headed. It isn’t enough to have data passing constantly through our servers; we need to be able to understand it using Telemetry. It isn’t enough to be able to pass packets through our DCs and WANs; we want to be able to route traffic using Segment Routing. Where we can, we want to use Automation to simplify our packages and deployments by limiting human interaction.
